Ognjen Stojanović

Ognjen Stojanović is a Serbian triathlete

Picture of Ognjen Stojanovic

Time line of Ognjen's life:

  • 1990 Born in Novi Sad, Serbia
  • 2005 Got involved in triathlon
  • 2006 National junior champion
  • 2007 National senior champion and Europian Junior Cup champion
  • 2008 European Junior Cup champion
  • 2009 Participate at Australian Younth Olimpic Festival, Balkan senior champion
  • 2011 Pan American continental cup champion
  • 2012 Third at the ITU World Aquathlon Championships
  • 2013 Founded the Triogy Racing club in Serbia, 6th on long distance triathlon with Arenales 113 race, 16th place on ETU European U23 Championships, National Marathon champion
  • 2014 Duatlon and triathlon Balkan champion, qualified for the 2015 European Games
  • 2015 18th on European Games, Balkan champion
  • 2016 2nd at ITU Asian Cup, ETU Balkan Championships Olimp champion

"Triathlon is my passion, outdoors my office."
